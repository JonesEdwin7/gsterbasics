International records

Under Dhoni's captaincy, India topped the Test cricket rankings for the first time, in 2009.[171] Dhoni has the most overseas Test defeats by an Indian captain, with 15.[172][173][174] Dhoni is the first Indian wicket-keeper to complete 4,000 Test runs.[175] Dhoni's maiden century against Pakistan in Faisalabad (148) is the fastest century scored by an Indian wicket-keeper, and fourth overall.[176] After hitting a six in the third Test against England in Southampton, Dhoni completed 50 sixes as a captain, an Indian record.[177] Dhoni, with 294 dismissals in his career, ranks first in the all-time dismissals list by Indian wicket-keepers.[178] Dhoni shares the record for most dismissals in an innings (6, with Syed Kirmani) and in a match (9) by an Indian wicket-keeper.[179] Dhoni is first Indian wicket-keeper to score 300 runs and take 15 dismissals in a series.[180]
